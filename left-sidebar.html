At AthenaAI, we are building a groundbreaking support system for stroke patients and their caregivers. Our platform combines advanced AI technology with a patient-centered approach to create an accessible, reliable, and personalized experience that truly meets the needs of each individual. Through AthenaAI, stroke patients and carers can engage with an intuitive AI-powered chat interface—offering both voice and text communication—that provides answers, guidance, and resources in real-time. Our marketplace brings together services and products curated to help patients and caregivers find the best solutions for rehabilitation, daily care, and overall well-being. AthenaAI aims to simplify the path forward with intelligent, compassionate, and customized support that adapts to every user's journey.

Our Story

AthenaAI was founded by a passionate team of experts in AI, product development, and startup growth. With firsthand experience in the tech and healthcare industries, we recognized the need for a solution that empowers stroke patients and their families with easy access to support and resources. Our journey began with the vision of building a platform that bridges the gap between technology and personal care—helping people recover and thrive in ways that are deeply meaningful and responsive to their needs. We believe in using technology to create not just functional tools, but a caring experience that eases the burden on carers and offers a sense of independence to patients. With AthenaAI, we aim to make the recovery journey smoother, more connected, and tailored to each individual.

Where are we now?

As a new and ambitious company, AthenaAI is working tirelessly to bring this vision to life. We’re developing a highly interactive AI interface capable of engaging users in natural, personalized conversations, designed specifically for the complexities of stroke care. Our marketplace is set to feature trusted services and products that align with the unique requirements of stroke recovery, offering both choice and convenience. With an unwavering commitment to quality and compassion, we’re excited to redefine what support looks like for stroke patients and their caregivers.
